Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

GreenThumb ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed and operated by Giacomo Matzeu as a solo developer based in Italy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the GreenThumb mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and optional display name. This is required to provide personalized plant care features.

Plant Data: Photos you take for plant identification and disease diagnosis, your plant collection details, care schedules, and watering history.

Location Data (approximate): With your permission, we collect approximate location to provide weather-aware plant care scheduling. You can disable this at any time in your device settings.

Community Content: If you use the community features, we collect the posts, comments, and interactions you create.

Calendar Data: If you enable calendar sync, the app creates watering reminder events on your device calendar. This data stays on your device.

Usage Data: We may collect crash logs and basic app interaction data to improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the GreenThumb app:

  • To authenticate your account and provide personalized features
  • To identify plants and diagnose diseases using AI
  • To generate smart watering schedules based on weather conditions
  • To enable community features (posts, comments, follows)
  • To process Pro subscription purchases
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted third-party services to provide app functionality. These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

Service Data Processed Purpose
Supabase Account data, plant data, community content Backend infrastructure, authentication, database
Plant.id Plant photos AI plant identification
Anthropic (Claude AI) Plant photos, descriptions AI disease diagnosis, care advisors
OpenWeatherMap Approximate location coordinates Weather data for care scheduling
RevenueCat User ID, purchase data Subscription management
Google Play Payment information Processing subscription payments

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only sent to the service providers listed above to deliver app functionality.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, all your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Certain data may be retained in encrypted backups for up to 90 days after deletion.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection (e.g., location)

To exercise any of these rights, use the account deletion / contact form.

8. Children's Privacy

GreenThumb is designed for a general audience aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
